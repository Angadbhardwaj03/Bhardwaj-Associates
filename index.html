Our Practice Areas

Comprehensive legal solutions across all tiers of the Indian judicial structure.

Constitutional & Appellate Law

Filing and defending Writ Petitions (Article 32 & 226), Public Interest Litigations (PILs), and Special Leave Petitions (SLPs) before the High Courts and the Supreme Court of India.

Civil Litigation & Dispute Resolution

Comprehensive representation in property disputes, breach of contract, recovery suits, injunctions, and specific relief matters across District and High Courts.

Criminal Defense & Trial

Strategic defense for white-collar crimes, bail applications (anticipatory and regular), quashing of FIRs, and full trial representation under the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) and allied laws.

Corporate & Commercial Law

Advisory and litigation for corporate compliance, mergers, shareholder disputes, and proceedings before the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) and NCLAT.

Family & Matrimonial Law

Sensitive and strategic handling of mutual and contested divorce, child custody, alimony, domestic violence cases, and family settlement matters.

Arbitration & Mediation (ADR)

Out-of-court dispute resolution, domestic and international commercial arbitration, and enforcement of arbitral awards to save time and legal costs.

Real Estate & Property Law

Title verification, RERA compliance and disputes, builder-buyer conflicts, and drafting of sale deeds and lease agreements.

Intellectual Property Rights (IPR)

Trademark, copyright, and patent registration, alongside aggressive litigation for IP infringement and passing off.

CAT Matters (Administrative Law)

Expert representation in Central Administrative Tribunal (CAT) cases, including service disputes, promotions, transfers, disciplinary actions, and government employee rights.
